Here are a few informations about Hitori's life:

  • Hitori Gotoh is the titular main protagonist of the manga and anime series, Bocchi the Rock!
  • She is in the first year of Shuka High School and is in charge of the guitar and lyrics of the band, Kessoku Band.
  • Her nickname Bocchi comes from the japanese word hitoribocchi, meaning "loneliness".
  • She started her guitar carrier with practicing for six hours every day.
  • She posts videos of her playing the guitar on the internet under the handle name "guitarhero".
  • Her first guitar is a 1968 Reissue Ebony. Her second model is a Yamaha PACIFICA611VFM.
